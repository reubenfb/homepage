Reuben Fischer-Baum

I’m an editor on the graphics team at The Washington Post. I previously worked at FiveThirtyEight and Deadspin, and was a 2021-2022 Nieman Fellow at Harvard. I teach data visualization at RISD and Brown, and experiment with webcam art.
