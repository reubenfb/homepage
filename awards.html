Classroom

I currently teach at both RISD and Brown University. Data Narratives (GRAPH 330G) is an elective for RISD graphic design MFA students, and Data Visualization & Storytelling (DATA 1500) is for undergrad and grad students at Brown as part of the Data Science Institute.

I previously taught Data Visualization & Information Aesthetic at Parsons School of Design from 2015-2017. I’ve lectured and led workshops at the Harvard Kennedy School, Harvard College, Columbia Journalism School, Columbia GSAPP, Tufts University, Skidmore College, Microsoft Research, and NYU.
